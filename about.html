Hi, I'm Sunwoo! Nice to meet you.

I'm a user experience designer seeking to create an empathetic digital experience that can genuinely enhance people’s lives. I am currently an MFA student in the Interaction Design program at the School of Visual Arts in New York.

Born and raised in Seoul, South Korea, I studied History, Art, and Technology at Sogang University. This experience allowed me to explore the many ways of using digital technology to represent society and people through visual art.
‍
I'm fascinated by the way UX design integrates cutting-edge technology, research, strategy, and narrative to solve pertinent problems. I believes this integration is key to solving problems and crafting significant solutions that can fundamentally shift people’s perspectives.

Outside of design, I am a huge moviegoer, about to reach 1000 movies ratings on the Letterboxd. I'm also an amateur photographer and loves video games.
