Problem Statement

How might we create a safe and inclusive environment that fosters emotional connections for individuals who are experiencing loneliness and isolation in New York City?

Secondary Research

From the research, my team and I learned that many of NYC residents feel loneliness regardless of age and genders. Hence during the pandemic, the number of people who feel solitude increased due to the lock down. We conducted 5 interviews from the people who are living in NYC and tried to learn about what makes them especially lonely in a huge city.
